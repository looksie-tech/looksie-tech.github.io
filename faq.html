WE ARE HERE FOR YOU

We built Looksie with you, the seller, in mind. If you have any questions or concerns that are not addressed in our FAQs below, drop us a line at info@letslooksie.com !

How do I create a seller account?

It’s super-fast and simple! Download the Looksie Seller app on the App Store and create an account. It should take you no more than 5 minutes. Once you’re done, you’ll be able to setup your store, list your products and use all the business tools available. Try it out!

I don’t have a business license… can I still sell on Looksie?

Absolutely! You don’t need to have a business license to sell on Looksie. Just upload an image of your ID during your account registration and you’ll be ready to go!

Why do you ask for my ID/business documents?

Our aim is to maintain a trusted marketplace for all buyers and sellers, and these documents will help us do just that!

What are the fees?

How will I get paid?

Payments will be initiated every Sunday for all deliveries that took place two weeks earlier.
For example:
Let’s say you made 7 deliveries between September 5 and September 11, totaling $70. We will initiate the payment for those deliveries ($70 minus fees) two weeks after September 11 – So the payment will be initiated on September 26th.

Let’s say you made another 7 deliveries between September 12 and September 18, totaling $70. We will initiate the payment for those deliveries two weeks after September 18th – So September 3rd (one week after your previous payment on September 26th).

You will continue to receive payments on a weekly basis.

What happens if I don’t agree with the amount deposited into my bank account?

You’ll have 7 days to dispute every payout by clicking on the dispute icon in the payouts screen (in profile settings), or you may contact us directly by email or phone. We will work with you to understand and solve any concerns you may have regarding your payouts.

What can I sell on Looksie?

You can sell pretty much anything as long as it does not violate our terms and conditions.
